About Us

Mocha Melt began with a simple vision: to bring soulful, handcrafted chocolate creations to those who crave authentic flavor. Every recipe is developed in-house, blending tradition with modern flair.

From single-origin cocoa to farm-fresh dairy, we partner with local purveyors who share our passion for sustainability. Our cozy shop invites you to slow down, savor, and celebrate the art of chocolate.
